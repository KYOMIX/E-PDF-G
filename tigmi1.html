Our Story

Welcome to Tigmino Maison D'or

Nestled in the heart of Tafraout, Tigmino Maison D'or is a traditional Berber guest house that offers an authentic Moroccan experience. Our property features a shared lounge, terrace, and offers free WiFi and private parking.

The surrounding area is popular for cycling, and bike rental is available at our inn. We take pride in preserving Berber heritage while providing modern comforts to our guests.

Our guest house is designed to reflect the rich cultural tapestry of Morocco, with traditional architecture, handcrafted details, and warm hospitality that will make your stay unforgettable.
