About Tigmino Maison D'or

A traditional Berber guest house where ancient hospitality meets modern comfort

A Taste of Berber Tradition

Located in the heart of Tafraout, Tigmino Maison D'or is a celebration of Berber culture and Moroccan hospitality. Our guest house is designed to provide you with an authentic experience while ensuring your comfort throughout your stay.

The property features a shared lounge where you can relax with other guests, a beautiful terrace to enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding landscapes, and free WiFi to stay connected. We also offer private parking for your convenience.
